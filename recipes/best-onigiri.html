Best Onigiri

Description

This is my personal favourite for when i have long trips or just a latenight guilty pleasure dish

Super easy, very cheap, and surprisingly delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Shiracha
  • Nori
  • Crab meat
  • Mayo
  • Spice
  • Kunzhut Oil

Steps

  1. Make rice.
  2. Cool rice.
  3. Break up crab meat and a few nori leaves.
  4. Dump everything in the pot in no particular order.
  5. Mix thoroughly.
  6. Make onigiri or balls of rice.
  7. EAT

As you can see this dish is very easy, very fast and very delicious.
